The Corebook

What's a DreadLore?

DreadLore is a story-telling tabletop roleplaying game (TTRPG) system  which is setting agnostic – you can use these rules for many different sorts of games,  Fantasy, Near future, Horror, Noir, Adventure, etc.

The DreadLore Corebook presents an original, Fantasy Punk setting – Craster.  We are also publishing various "Splat" books for different settings, at present, a near  future setting called "Into Space."  If you enjoy taking a role in the  creative process of story-telling, and you also wanna roll some dice and kill bad  (or good) guys…chances are – this is the system for you.

What's a TTRPG?

Ever heard of a Table-Top RolePlaying Game (TTRPG)? e.g. Dungeons & Dragons,  World of Darkness, Numenera, etc.? DreadLore is a similar sort of board game. These  games are classified in several ways: table-top games, pen & ink, RPGs, and silly  dice games.

Most systems are comprised of a 'Setting' (sci-fi, fantasy, etc.) and a list of  'Core Mechanics' (how the numbers & math work in the game). TTRPGs also require  a storyteller, called an Arbiter in DreadLore, in addition to 2-6 Players to play.

Mechanics

Characters Forge the Story

  • Dive into the Story

    DreadLore offers two captivating modes: The Dread and The Montage.

  • The Dread: Take Control

    In The Dread, players seize the narrative reins. They Bid, roll dice, and engage character mechanics, shaping the story through their choices. It's a thrilling high-wire act where success and failure hang in the balance.

  • Dynamic Dice Mechanics: The Throw Down

    During a "Throw Down," players aim for [Successes], counting them individually. Rolls of 4-7 secure 1 [Success], while 8+ brings 2 [Successes].

  • Survive the Challenge

    DreadLore is high-risk, low-vitality gameplay. Characters have limited [Plot Armor], determined by their choices. When it depletes, players allocate damage to Wounds or Sanity. Hit zero in either, and death or permanent insanity looms.

  • Dynamic Turn Order

    DreadLore's turn order adds depth:
    1. World Action: A round begins with "World Action," sparking unpredictability as all react to unfolding events.
    2. Cunning Turn: Players can seize initiative by spending Cunning for two turns, enabling tactical moves.
    3. Antagonist's Turn: Antagonists challenge characters and drive plot twists.
    4. Player's Turn: Here, choices define character fate, influenced by prior actions.

  • Versatile Storytelling

    DreadLore transcends genres, from horror to adventure, near-future to fantasy, offering creative freedom.

  • Your Way

    DreadLore celebrates your creativity. Craft the stories you crave, whether exploring haunted mansions, solving mysteries, or venturing into fantasy realms.

  • Endless Possibilities

    Imagination knows no bounds in DreadLore. Dive into thrilling adventures and intricate tales, adapting to your vision.

  • Enhanced Gameplay

    Players access Resources for bonuses, rerolls, and Extra Dice, detailed in the Getting Started Guide (GSG) and DreadLore Corebook.

    • Join DreadLore where limitless storytelling unfolds, choices reverberate, rules guide, and character journeys shape epic adventures. Traverse uncharted territories of immersive storytelling, where the only limit is your imagination!
